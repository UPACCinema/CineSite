Membership Guidelines

All members will meet requirements of the film series they have joined. A show is a single showing of a film, from the time it starts to the time it ends. A movie is a group of shows whether on a single day or many consectuive days.

Requirements are as follows:

Friday Night Pop Films 2 shows at each movie that the team is to show, 8 shows total per semester
Midweek Films 6 shows out of the 10 shown each semester
Repertory Films 6 shows out of the 10 shown each semester
Publicity 10 weeks out of the semester putting up advertisement materials

  • Each member must stay for the last hour show at least ONCE during the semester.
  • If a member is unable to work on night, time must be made up. Time may be made up by working other shows or other work at the series coordinator's discretion.
  • All members (regardless of date joined) must fulfill all requirements in order to receive all privileges
  • All penalties shall be at the discretion of the series coordinator.
